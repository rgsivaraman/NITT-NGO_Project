Organisational structure

Need for Needy believes in multiplying its efforts through collaboration & partnerships that bring together government, non-government, and corporate in a collaborative process for socio-economic development, improving agriculture, environment, health, and women empowerment. We put a lot of emphasis on upgrading the skills and capacities of its staff. For this purpose, need-based training is regularly organized

Vision

We aim to create a development revolution for the marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society.

Mission

Our mission is the creation of such a society where nobody is deprived of the main current of development. We are determined for the establishment of that society in which everybody possesses ‘Human rights’ with dignity.
